Welcome to Brikama Methodist Academy

Brikama Methodist Academy is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering a love for learning in every student. Our experienced teachers and modern facilities create an environment where students thrive academically and personally.

Mission Statement

Under God we seek to provide quality and affordable education espacially for children in Brikama and the environs.

Vision Statement

Through wholesome discipline producing the citizen rsponsible to himself/herself, his or her community and nation.
